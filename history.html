Formation

     

Coldplay are a British rock band formed in 1996. The band was originaly named Pectoralz, before Guy Berryman joined, later; they changed their name to Starfish. Will Champion joined as drummer and backing vocalist, The band renamed themselves "Coldplay" in 1998.

Jonny Buckland, on Guitar.

Guy Berryman, on Bass.

Will Champion, Backing Vocolist and Drums.
